The Practice

Materiality, light, and volumetric clarity.

form + space is an architectural studio operating at the intersection of spatial volume, tactile craft, and atmospheric purity. We believe buildings and objects should converse quietly with their surrounding environment.

Led by Natasya Handajuwana and Ziwei Zhu, our methodology grounds conceptual rigor into built reality through precise digital fabrication and physical prototyping.

Natasya Handajuwana

Co-Founder & Principal Architect

Specializes in spatial narrative, conceptual programming, and volumetric layout orchestration across civic and residential commissions.

Ziwei Zhu

Co-Founder & Design Director

Focuses on material experimentation, detail articulation, and integrated digital-to-physical tectonic fabrication.
