Natasya Handajuwana

I am an interior designer at heart, but my work focuses on the dialogue where interiors and architecture meet. Having developed my practice from Milan to Tokyo—most recently serving as a senior designer at Sou Fujimoto Architects—I specialty in bridging detailed interior spaces with large-scale architectural design. My approach balances global concepts with deeply local traditions, focusing on how spaces come to life through human interaction. I thrive on collaborating across different cultures and minds to create meaningful, human-centric environments for our clients.

2011 - 2015 Bachelor of Interior Architecture at Universitas Pelita Harapan, Indonesia
2016 - 2016 Master of Interior Design at Istituto Europeo di Design, Italy
2017 - 2026 Senior Designer at Sou Fujimoto Architects in Tokyo, Japan
2019 Notable project: Renovation of Shiroiya Hotel in Gunma, Japan

Ziwei Zhu

With my passion for architecture, nature and humans I explore the spatial experiences which allows one to become their true selves. Having a multicultural background allows me to transition easily between global modern architecture and true local design practices.

My personal hobbies are walking through nature and some natural photography which captures memories and moments which are more intimate.

2010 - 2017 Bachelors and Masters of Architecture at University of Technology at Delft
2017 - 2018 Junior Architect at KAAN architects in Rotterdam, the Netherlands
2018 - 2026 Senior Designer at Sou Fujimoto Architects in Tokyo, Japan
2023 Winner of the Starchitects Chair design competition
2025 Golden Mention finalist for Living Ruins II design competition
